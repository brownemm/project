Story time. Hi. My name is Emma Brown. Let me tell you a story. Last year, my family decided to go sledding. We don't have enough pairs of boots for each of us, so my sister borrowed a pair from the neighbors. After we picked the boots up, my sister began to put them on, but the sole felt all bunched up. So, she reached her hand in to pull out the sole, and instead pulled out A DEAD MOUSE! She squealed and screamed. But that's not the end of the story. My mom texted our neighbor and related the funny story to her. Our neighbor talked to her daughter, who owned the boots. She told her mom that she had felt the misplaced sole the last time she had worn them, but hadn't bothered to fix it. But worst of all--she hadn't worn any socks!!
